Who we are

Prudent is a Composite Insurance Broker regulated by Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI). At Prudent, we focus on building relationships and the confidence of our partners. As a partner-focused company, we provide clients with unparalleled, best-in-class services.

As one of the leading insurance brokers headquartered in Mumbai, India, we have strategic partners in over 120 countries to service your insurance needs. Having mastered the art of understanding, interpreting, and pre-empting the different kinds of risks that our clients face, we have become truly empowered, and in return, we empower them with confidence even during uncertain times.

We have equipped ourselves with extensive resources that enable us to reinvent the Indian insurance landscape, providing clients with path-breaking and future-facing risk management programs based on global best practices.

Our Vision

Prudent is committed to being a leading player in shaping the future of the insurance industry in India. We wish to be recognized as the standard to which other intermediaries aspire.

Prudent aims to be the most respected insurance broker in the country by acting with utmost good faith with clients and underwriters and leading in technical competence, service delivery, productivity and profitability per team member, and adopting modern tools for delivering the most efficient and valued service possible.

We will be a pan-Indian broker with regional and global connections that enhance the value of the advice and services we provide to our clients.

Our Mission

We are in a people-oriented service driven industry. Prudent will create the most productive and rewarding work environment in the Indian insurance broking industry. Our team members will possess technical competence; zeal and vigor for delivering world-class service to our clients, and mutual respect and teamwork that will be second to none.

Our clients will be assured of consistency and quality in our solution delivery which will be evaluated and monitored to ensure that every client receives our very best efforts. We will participate in the shaping of the Indian insurance industry to ensure that it develops effectively with special emphasis on the rights of the insured.
